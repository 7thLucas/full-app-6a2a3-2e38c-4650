The Problem

Compliance tracking is manual,
fragmented, and exam-failing

Expired certificates go unnoticed — discovered only when a regulator asks

HR spends hours each week manually chasing documents via email

No unified view across departments — scorecards are outdated spreadsheets

Escalation chains are informal — non-compliance persists weeks with no audit trail

Sales, Compliance/Risk, and HR each follow different rules with no shared framework

The Solution

One platform that automates the full compliance lifecycle

Employees see a real-time color-coded scorecard and upload docs in one click

HR configures rules per department, role, and document — flexible and enforceable

Automated alerts at 90, 30, and 7 days before expiry — and every 7 days after

Structured escalation: Manager → HR → CCO with timestamps and attribution

CSV/PDF export on demand — audit-ready in minutes, not preparation days
